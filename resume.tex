\subsection{}\label{section}

Michael Kelleman is a Senior Statistician II with over a decade of
combined academic and industry experience, specializing in real-world
evidence, clinical research, and advanced statistical methodologies. He
brings extensive expertise in R, SAS, SQL, and CDISC standards, applying
robust analytical techniques across cardiology, emergency medicine,
nephrology, and autoimmune disease research. Michael has led large
multicentre projects, authored numerous scientific publications, and
consistently delivers high-quality, reproducible statistical solutions.
